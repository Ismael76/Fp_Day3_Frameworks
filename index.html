Pizza Makes Anything Possible.

In Sardinia, French and Italian archaeologists have found bread baked over 7,000 years ago. According to Philippe Marinval, the local islanders leavened this bread. Foods similar to pizza have been made since antiquity. Records of people adding other ingredients to bread to make it more flavorful can be found throughout ancient history. Nowdays due to globalisation we can see how pizza has impacted on foods ranging from Pedi to Zapiekanki and many more.
